PROTECTING FACILITIES AROUND THE WORLD WITH EFFECTIVE SECURITY DESIGNS

CloudNext offers the experience and global resources to provide Fortune 1000 companies with reliable and comprehensive Security Design Services for a wide variety of facilities around the world.

CloudNext’s Security Design Consultants can be incorporated at any stage in your facility’s development. Our team can have great impact during the architectural concept design or construction implementation stage, or while commissioning interior fit-outs.

CloudNext is meticulous in the way we approach the security design process. Our approach entails rigorous design plans to ensure mitigation of risks – such as incorporating Crime Prevention Through Environment Design (CPTED) – followed by diligent assessment of the concept.     

CloudNext delivers the following essential components with each security design:

  • Complex and Floor Layout – Design study from safety and security view point
  • Security Ancillaries – illumination needs, security signage, tech infrastructure
  • Access Controls – vehicle, pedestrian, and material access check points
  • CCTV Operations – optimizing equipment and locations
  • Tactical Positioning of Security Guards
  • Documentation – including Security Policy, Standard Operating Procedures and C-Cube Operations Manual
  • Training Recommendations
  • Testing and Selection Procedures
  • Security Guard Equipment Recommendations
  • Intruder Detection & Monitoring – safeguarding non-occupied areas
  • Command & Control Centre (C-Cube) – alarm and feed integration, communication relay and layout
  • Security Function Organogram – structure, electioning and training teams
